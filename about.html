About
The vision behind Experience XR was to give VR and XR gaming enthusiasts a space to come together, meet and learn about the changes and future of the industry. The purpose of having it in a VR space is make using VR technology more normalized. Additionally it gives companies and experts a chance to showcase their work remotely and to get everyone excited!
